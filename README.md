# HR Insights Pro - Intelligent Workforce Analytics Dashboard

![Project Banner](https://via.placeholder.com/1200x500/0A3D2E/00FF9F?text=HR+Insights+Pro)

**Turning HR Data into Strategic Business Intelligence**

---

## 📋 Business Problem

Organizations often struggle with **reactive HR decision-making** due to fragmented data, lack of visibility into talent risks, and delayed insights on promotion and retention issues.

**HR Insights Pro** was built to solve this by providing **real-time, actionable workforce intelligence** that enables HR leaders and executives to make faster, data-driven decisions.

---

## ✨ Project Overview

**HR Insights Pro** is a clean, modern, and highly interactive **Power BI dashboard** that transforms raw HR data into clear strategic insights. It focuses on **Talent Management, Risk Identification, and Executive Decision Support**.

---

## 🎯 Key Business Insights

- **92%** of the workforce is currently active, but **117 employees (8%)** have been flagged for retrenchment — with **Research & Development** accounting for **74 employees** (highest risk area).
- **72 employees** are due for promotion, with the **Manager** role having the largest backlog (22 employees).
- **84.63%** of employees are rated High Performers, showing strong overall talent quality.
- Majority of the workforce is concentrated in **Job Level 1 & 2**, indicating a need for clear career progression pathways.
- Strong job satisfaction with **569 employees** in the High category.
